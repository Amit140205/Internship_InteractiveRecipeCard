Chocolate Lava Cake

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 12 min
Servings: 4
Ready to start
  • 4 oz dark chocolate, chopped
  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • Pinch of salt
  • Butter for ramekins
  • Cocoa powder for dusting
1
Preheat oven to 425°F. Butter four 6-oz ramekins and dust with cocoa powder.
2
Melt chocolate and butter in a double boiler until smooth. Let cool slightly.
3
Whisk eggs and sugar until thick and pale, about 3 minutes.
4
Fold in melted chocolate mixture, then add flour and salt.
5
Divide batter among ramekins and bake for 12-14 minutes.
6
Let cool for 1 minute, then run knife around edges and invert onto plates.
